### 1. **University & Scholarship Matcher**

**Goal:** Save users time by aggregating and filtering global university and scholarship information.

**Context:**
Vietnamese students face fragmented and biased information when searching for study-abroad opportunities. The system helps them **quickly find suitable universities or scholarships** based on their profile and preferences, providing transparent reasoning for each match.

**Core Functions:**

* Centralized database of universities and scholarships (basic info, location, tuition, requirements).
* Filters: major, GPA, budget, language, country, ranking.
* Basic matching algorithm calculating a **fit score** per university/scholarship using user profile data.
* Clear UI: fit percentage and reasons for match displayed on each card.

**Outcome:**
Users obtain a personalized shortlist of universities/scholarships aligned with their background and goals, reducing research time and decision fatigue.

---

### 2. **Application Dashboard**

**Goal:** Centralize the management of target universities and application progress.

**Context:**
Students often lose track of application requirements and deadlines. The dashboard provides a **lightweight overview** of their goals and progress without introducing unnecessary complexity.

**Core Functions:**

* Add universities from the Matcher to a personal dashboard.
* Overview of all selected universities: fit score, deadlines, and progress stage.
* Simple progress tracker: “Not Started / In Progress / Submitted.”
* Optional reminder system (in-app or email).

**Outcome:**
Users can **track application progress**, visualize deadlines, and maintain control over their study-abroad plan.

---

### 3. **Persona Lab / MyLeap (Essay & Self-Discovery Assistant)**

**Goal:** Help users explore their identity, reflect on achievements, and generate essay ideas and profile insights.

**Context:**
Students often struggle to connect academic and extracurricular achievements into a coherent personal narrative. Persona Lab guides **self-discovery and essay ideation** while laying the groundwork for future profile improvement features.

**Core Functions:**

* Interactive question prompts (motivation, challenges, achievements, values).
* AI processes responses to suggest potential **essay angles, central narratives, and improvement ideas**.
* Generates basic outlines or idea sketches (not full essays).
* Iterative refinement: users can update answers and receive updated insights.
* Future extensions: profile growth suggestions, visualization of strengths/weaknesses, and roadmap for skill/experience development.

**Outcome:**
Users gain clarity on their personal story and essay direction, improve confidence, and have actionable insights for enhancing their study-abroad profile.

---

### Key Notes on MVP Focus

* **Efficiency first:** Matcher + Dashboard should minimize manual research and tracking.
* **Exploration & guidance:** Persona Lab emphasizes discovery and reflection, forming the “AI companion / mentor” element of the platform.
* **Scalable future features:** Persona Lab is designed to evolve from essay ideation → full profile coaching → holistic study-abroad guidance.
